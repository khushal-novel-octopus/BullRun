Gym Trainer

We need a certified trainer who can inspire members and deliver results. You'll be the driving force on the gym floor, responsible for creating personalized programs and fostering a high-energy environment.

Core Responsibilities:
  • Conduct member fitness assessments and consultations.
  • Design and oversee safe and effective training routines.
  • Lead small group training sessions and workshops.
What We're Looking For:
  • Recognized personal training certification.
  • Deep understanding of exercise physiology.
  • Excellent interpersonal and motivational skills.

Front Desk Manager

As the first point of contact, you are the face of BullRun Fitness Club. We're seeking a friendly, organized individual to manage memberships, handle inquiries, and ensure a seamless experience for every member.

Core Responsibilities:
  • Greet members and manage club check-ins.
  • Process new memberships, renewals, and payments.
  • Answer phone calls and respond to email inquiries promptly.
What We're Looking For:
  • Proven experience in a customer service role.
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • A positive attitude and a passion for helping people.

Finance Manager

We are looking for a detail-oriented Finance Manager to oversee our club's financial health. You will handle budgeting, financial reporting, and payroll to ensure our operations run smoothly.

Core Responsibilities:
  • Manage accounts payable/receivable and payroll.
  • Prepare monthly and quarterly financial statements.
  • Develop budgets and financial forecasts.
What We're Looking For:
  • Bachelor's degree in Finance or Accounting.
  • Experience with accounting software.
  • Strong analytical skills.
